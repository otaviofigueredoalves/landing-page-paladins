What is Paladins?

Paladins is a captivating free-to-play game that invites you to become a Champion of the Realm. Let me break it down for you:

Champions:

In Paladins, you step into the shoes of powerful champions, each with their unique abilities and playstyles. Whether you prefer sniping foes from a distance, tossing grenades, or sprinting around with an assault rifle, there’s a champion for every taste.

Deckbuilding System

What sets Paladins apart is its flexible deckbuilding system. As a champion, you can customize your abilities by selecting cards from a vast pool. These cards enhance your skills, allowing you to tailor your playstyle. Imagine being an iron sights sniper one moment and a grenade-slinging expert the next—all within the same champion!

Game Modes

  • Siege: Engage in epic battles where teams vie for control points and push payloads.
  • Team Deathmatch: Fast-paced combat where eliminations matter most.
  • Onslaught: A mode focused on capturing objectives and maintaining control.
